About

My research focuses on developing AI-driven methods to support robust decision-making in complex operational systems. Specifically, I work on statistical and machine learning approaches that capture structural changes, quantify uncertainty, and model rare or safety-critical events. Applications of my work span healthcare operations, energy systems, and transportation.
